🍱 Taste of Food Through Ky 🦐

A food gallery and review of foods from everywhere.

Welcome to A Taste of Food Through Ky. This site will give a gallery of some of the different foods I have eaten, along with my personal rating of each of them.

As a certified "foodie", it is in my blood to capture the moment and allow my camera to eat first. Throughout the years, some of the best memories I have made have been trying new foods.

From one food lover to another, nothing beats the feeling of trying a new food that leaves such an impression that you will never forget about.
