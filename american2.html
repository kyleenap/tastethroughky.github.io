American Cuisine

Although "American Cuisine" can be defined as many different things, I would consider it to be anything that not culturally-specific to one certain group of people. Different foods such as: burgers, hot dogs, steak, etc. would all be considered as "American" in my book. Food, like pizza, can be put under this umbrella, as there is a clear distinction between pizza made in the United States versus pizza made in Italy.

Check out this list of foods that are generally considered American cuisine.

Foods I Have Tried
Burger Pizza Steak
Burgers are a classic, as you can almost never go wrong with them. This one contained a crispy bacon and fried onion interior, which complimented the fresh vegetables inside incredibly well! This pizza was a beautiful take on a classic, oven-roasted pizza. It had all sorts of delicious toppings, including shrimp and a spicy, savory sauce that put it all together. For someone that does not typically eat steak, this one was extremely tender and cooked to perfection. The classic pairing of steak with lobster and the side of vegetables made for an amazingly balanced meal.
Southern Pasta Smothered Burrito
This was one of the first times I tried blackended fish. Although it was different from the other ways I have tried fish before, the flavor profile was very unique and I felt it was very delicious, especially considering the fact that it is a historical and local dish to Southern areas of the United States, like New Orleans. This seafood pasta brought the taste of shrimp and squid together like a melody of flavors. The saltiness of the seafood with the creamy pasta sauce made for an extremely savory but light dish. Smothered burritos are a Colorado-local dish, as they are smothered in a chili verde sauce that contains pork. Since most other states in the U.S. do not grow green chiles, that is was makes this dish so unique. As someone that loves spicy food and does not live in Colorado, this hits the spot every time I visit. The flavors that come with this burrito are so unique, and you cannot find them in any other dish.
